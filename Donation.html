Shri Krishna Janmashtami

Though unborn and eternal, the Supreme Lord, of His free will and causeless mercy, incarnates to deliver the pious and annihilate the miscreants. Lord Krishna appeared as the son of Vasudeva and Devaki on the midnight of janmashtmi Janmashtami is celebrated very gorgeously in ISKCON Pandharpur.

Radha Pandharinath Nitya Seva

A devotee offers everything to Krishna – his words, his actions, and his thoughts. To give something to Krishna is an extremely personal and elevated activity.

Go-seva

Cows are most auspicious animals. They are very dear to Lord Krishna. Everything of the cow including urine and dung is beneficial to human society.

Gita Daan
