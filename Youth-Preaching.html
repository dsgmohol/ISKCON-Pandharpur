Thoughts

~Albert Einstein

"When doubts haunt me, when disappointments stare me in the face, and I see not one ray of hope on the horizon, I turn to Bhagavad-Gita and find a verse to comfort me; and I immediately begin to smile in the midst of overwhelming sorrow. What do we :

The Prabhupada Ghat project will be beneficial in many ways :
– Practice of lifestyle with high Values and Principle.
– Learning and Sharing for our academic/Career development.
– Ultimate Service -Serving Krishna.
– Stay together pray together .
– Mantra meditation for reviewing our original pure nature.
